About the blog

In the past year, I have read many excellent blogs written by inspiring individuals. I also hope to document things that interest me to see if I can inspire myself or others who read my blog.

The name of the blog comes from: when we find bugs, we always say that something seems wrong here. Finding what’s wrong seems to be the beginning of success.

In daily work and study, it is also the case that when we find something seems wrong, that is when we can make improvements.

I hope everyone can notice things that seems wrong in one’s life, then follow the clues to find the root causes, fix the problems, and achieve self-improvement.
